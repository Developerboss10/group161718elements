Topic 3

O3 vs SO2

O3 — Oxidising

Decomposes: O3 → O2 + [O]. Bleaches permanently.

SO2 — Reducing

Oxidised to SO3/H2SO4. Bleaches temporarily.

"O3 aag laavto, SO2 pani sambhalt"

[2024]O3 = oxidising[2026]SO2 = reducing

Group 16 — Quick Facts

O anomaly: No d-orbitals. OS: -2, -1, -1/2, +2 only. Max covalency = 2.

Hydrides: Acidity up down group. H2O highest BP (H-bonding).

O3 = strong oxidiser. SO2 = reducing agent.

Group 17 — Quick Facts

Oxidising: F2 > Cl2 > Br2 > I2 (F2 strongest)

EGE: Cl > F > Br > I (Cl highest, not F!)

HX Acidity: HI > HBr > HCl > HF (HF weakest)

HF BP: Highest due to H-bonding

Displacement: Higher oxidiser displaces lower
